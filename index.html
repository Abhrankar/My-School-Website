What's New

  • Covid-19 Pandemic

    The COVID-19 crisis is the latest global challenge and we need to work together to slow the spread. As a Network of Schools spread across India, we take National and Global Health very seriously. As per the Government Guidelines, all Ryan International Schools in India have been closed until further notice. All our Schools are also taking steps to protect the health and wellbeing of our community in line with World Health Organization (WHO) recommendations. Thank you for your patience in such difficult circumstances.

  • E-Learning

    Virtual School during Covid 19 : With the outbreak of the pandemic of Covid-19 the world was faced with a situation where only those who adapted survived. Ryan Education Network was quick at adapting to the situation. In the short transition period from pre-lockdown to lockdown Ryan Education Network collaborated with Toppr OS on the 2nd of April, 2020 - the scheduled day for the start of the new academic session.
